### Preassumptions
Crude Estimator. (Ref. [1])
1. Data from interval `\left[a,b\right]`
2. Number of observations is `n`, Number of patitions is `J`, each of length `h=(b-a)/J`. (Assumption: partitions identical)
Then, Crude estimator is `\hat{f_{j}}=\frac{n_{j}}{nh}`, where `n_{j}` denote number of observations appearing in interval `j`.
